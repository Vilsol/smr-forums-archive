Hi, i found way to solve the problem for me. After installing SMM and the mods i want, i get the same error message. CL151248 SML v3.0.0 Then i copied the SML binaries (*.dll, *.pdb) from …\Satisfactory\FactoryGame\Mods\SML\Binaries\Win64 To …\Satisfactory\FactoryGame\Binaries\Win64 After that i modified the file UE4-Win64-Shipping.modules and add the SML Modul “SML”: “UE4-SML-Win64-Shipping.dll” Cheers
